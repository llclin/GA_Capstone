COMPASS C6. Strategic Economic Priorities (SEP) bonus - eligible programmes

To be considered for the C6. SEP bonus, your organisation needs to participate in at least one of the eligible programmes.

Eligible SEP programmes

Your organisation needs to participate in at least one of the following programmes to be considered for the C6. SEP bonus.

  • Download the eligible SEP programmes.
  • Contact the supporting agencies directly if you have any queries.
Supporting agency SEP programmes and economic criteria

Economic Development Board (EDB)

EDB_COMPASS@edb.gov.sg
  • Development and Expansion Incentive (DEI), including International Headquarters-DEI
  • Pioneer Certificate Incentive (PC)
  • Research and Innovation Scheme for Companies (RISC)
  • Economic criteria: Manufacturer with significant activities in Singapore (for example, it must hire at least 500 locals)

Enterprise Singapore (EnterpriseSG)

ESG_COMPASS@esg.gov.sg
  • Global Trader Programme (GTP)
  • Scale-Up SG
  • Singapore Global Executive Programme (SGEP)
  • Economic criteria: High-growth startup that was incorporated in Singapore with a minimum investment amount of USD$10 million over the past 36 months, and received funding from recognised investment organisations

Infocomm Media Development Authority (IMDA)

IMDA_COMPASS@imda.gov.sg
  • Accreditation@SG Digital
  • SG:D Spark

Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore (MPA)

MPA_COMPASS@mpa.gov.sg
  • Maritime Sector Incentive:
    • International Shipping Enterprise (MSI-AIS) Award
    • Shipping-related Support Services (MSI-SSS) Award
    • Maritime Leasing (ML) Award
  • Maritime Cluster Fund – Business Development (MCF-BD)

Singapore Tourism Board (STB)

STB_COMPASS@stb.gov.sg
  • Selected Business Improvement Fund (BIF) grantee with innovative projects that bring about substantial transformation or productivity savings
  • Selected Singapore Tourism Accelerator (STA) participant with innovative projects that bring about substantial transformation or productivity savings

National Trades Union Congress (NTUC)

askme@ntuc.org.sg

Be a progressive organisation that is committed to work with the Labour Movement to strengthen local workforce development through either:

  • NTUC’s Company Training Committees (CTCs)
  • Participation in Government-supported programmes
