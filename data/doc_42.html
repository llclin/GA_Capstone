Must the job in the EP or S Pass application match the job advertised on the MyCareersFuture?

Yes, it needs to match the one advertised on MyCareersFuture.

Each job advertisement on MyCareersFuture will have a Job Advertisement ID issued by WSG. You need to provide the Job Advertisement ID when applying for an Employment Pass or S Pass.
